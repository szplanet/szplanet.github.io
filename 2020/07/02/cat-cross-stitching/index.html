Cat Cross-Stitching

Am I A Crazy Cat Lady Yet?

Posted by Sherry Zhang on Thursday, July 2, 2020

Table of Content

I love making things with my hands and cross-stitching has always been something that I wanted to try. During the past couple months, I had time to take a pause in the weekly travels, so I picked up a cross-stitching kit. As a “crazy cat lady”, I decided to go with this cat pattern. At the time of purchase, I thought to myself that I can totally knock this off in a couple days because it looked like a straightforward beginner level kit. Little did I know, the subtly in the colors and the precision of each stitch required high attention to details and little room for errors. There were a couple times that I messed up the count or the position of the stitch, so I had to rewind and start over. I organized my workflow by colors and area – I started with the white in the middle, then grey and black on each side, and finally the smaller green, pink, and white remaining areas. As an Industrial Engineer by training, I tried to optimize each thread by using an efficient path and minimize waste. Finally, after 2 weeks and 100+ hours of work, I finished this “Meow”! It gave me a great sense of accomplishment and a cute piece of art in my room.
